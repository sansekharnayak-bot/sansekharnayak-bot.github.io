Biography

Born: 19 July 1957 — Eminent Odiya poet celebrated for his evocative poetry and contributions to Odia literature.

Shatrughna Pandab’s work reflects a deep engagement with social, spiritual, and personal themes. His poems bridge tradition and modernity, using lyrical language that resonates with readers across generations. Below is a timeline of some notable milestones.

1957

Born on 19 July 1957.

1970s–1980s

Early poetry publications and recognition in local literary circles.

1990s–2000s

Major collections released and invitations to national poetry festivals.

2010s–Present

Continued readings, interviews and expanded multimedia presence.

Legacy & Impact
